Welm is a to-do app for people who actually want to finish things.

Add tasks. Do them. Unlock room for more.

Repeat until you run out of things or excuses. No tags. No integrations. No algorithm deciding what matters.

Just a list, your choices, and whatever willpower you’ve managed to hold onto.

It does have one flaw however.

It’s ugly. We’re calling it a feature.

Try it anyway.
