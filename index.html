Welm is a to-do list system for humans who actually want to finish things.

Add tasks. Do them. Unlock room for more. Repeat until you run out of things or excuses.

No tagging. No integrations. No algorithm deciding what matters. Just a list, your choices, and some simple rules to build your willpower.

Welm’s sole purpose is to help you build and maintain agency.

Which unfortunately means it can’t afford to distract you with good looks.
