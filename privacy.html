Privacy Policy

Last updated: Sep 13, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy applies to Welm (the "App") developed by Jerry Chang (the "Developer"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or information from users of this App.

The App:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Does not track user behavior or analytics
  • Does not access device information beyond what is necessary for basic app functionality
  • Does not share any information with third parties

Local Data Storage

Any data generated or used by the App (such as user preferences or app settings) is stored locally on your device only. This information:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to the Developer
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the App

Third-Party Services

This App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, this App is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change in future updates, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the App Store update description.

Contact Information

This privacy policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of the App.
